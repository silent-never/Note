$$
\left. \begin{aligned} 
\frac{\mathrm{d}\dot{U}}{\mathrm{d}x} &= -(r_0 + \mathrm{j}\omega l_0)\dot{I} \\ 
\frac{\mathrm{d}\dot{I}}{\mathrm{d}x} &= -(g_0 + \mathrm{j}\omega c_0)\dot{U} 
\end{aligned} \right\}
$$

$$
\frac{\mathrm{d}^2\dot{U}}{\mathrm{d}x^2}= (r_0 + \mathrm{j}\omega l_0)(g_0 + \mathrm{j}\omega c_0)\dot{U} =\tilde{\gamma}^2\dot{U}
$$

$$
\dot U=\dot A_U \mathrm{ch}(\tilde{\gamma} x)+\dot B_U \mathrm{sh}(\tilde{\gamma} x)
$$

同理有：
$$
\dot I=\dot A_I \mathrm{ch}(\tilde{\gamma} x)+\dot B_I \mathrm{sh}(\tilde{\gamma} x)
$$

边界条件：
$$
\left. \begin{aligned} 
\dot{U}|_{x=0} &=\dot U_1 \\ 
\dot{I}|_{x=0} &=\dot I_1
\end{aligned} \right\}
$$

有：
$$
\left. \begin{alignedat}{2}
\dot U&=\dot U_1 \mathrm{ch}(\tilde{\gamma} x)&&+\dot B_U \mathrm{sh}(\tilde{\gamma} x) \\ 
\dot I&=\dot I_1 \mathrm{ch}(\tilde{\gamma} x)&&+\dot B_I  \mathrm{sh}(\tilde{\gamma} x)
\end{alignedat} \right\}
$$

代入一阶导数关系:
$$
\left. \begin{alignedat}{2}
\tilde{\gamma} \dot U_1 &= -(r_0 + \mathrm{j}\omega l_0)\dot B_I \\ 
\tilde{\gamma} \dot B_U &= -(r_0 + \mathrm{j}\omega l_0)\dot I_1
\end{alignedat} \right\}
$$

取：
$$
\tilde{Z_c}=\sqrt{\frac{r_0 + \mathrm{j}\omega l_0}{g_0 + \mathrm{j}\omega c_0}}=\frac{r_0 + \mathrm{j}\omega l_0}{\tilde{\gamma}}
$$

有：
$$
\left. \begin{alignedat}{2}
\dot U&=\dot U_1 \mathrm{ch}(\tilde{\gamma} x) &&- \tilde{Z_c}\dot I_1 \mathrm{sh}(\tilde{\gamma} x) \\ 
\dot I&=\dot I_1 \mathrm{ch}(\tilde{\gamma} x) &&- \frac{\dot U_1}{\tilde{Z_c}}  \mathrm{sh}(\tilde{\gamma} x)
\end{alignedat} \right\}
$$

化简成二端口网络为：
$$
\begin{bmatrix} 
\dot U_2 \\ 
\dot I_2 
\end{bmatrix} = 
\begin{bmatrix} 
\mathrm{ch}(\tilde{\gamma} x) &-\tilde{Z_c} \mathrm{sh}(\tilde{\gamma} x) \\[4pt] 
-\frac{\mathrm{sh}(\tilde{\gamma} x)}{\tilde{Z_c}}   & \mathrm{ch}(\tilde{\gamma} x)
\end{bmatrix} 
\begin{bmatrix} 
\dot{U}_1 \\ 
\dot{I}_1
\end{bmatrix} 
$$

波速为：（需要在非相量法的微分方程中导出，并且需要忽略损耗）
$$
v=\frac{1}{\sqrt{l_0c_0}}=\frac{c}{n}
$$
称 $Z_c$ 为特征阻抗。

对于无损线，方程退化为：
$$
\tilde{\gamma}=\sqrt{-\omega^2l_0c_0}=j\omega \sqrt{l_0c_0}=jk,\quad Z_c=\sqrt{\frac{l_0}{c_0}}
$$

$$
\begin{bmatrix} 
\dot U_2 \\ 
\dot I_2 
\end{bmatrix} = 
\begin{bmatrix} 
\cos(k x) &-jZ_c \sin(k x) \\[4pt] 
-j\frac{\sin(k x)}{Z_c}   & \cos(k x)
\end{bmatrix} 
\begin{bmatrix} 
\dot{U}_1 \\ 
\dot{I}_1
\end{bmatrix} 
$$

注意到行列式为1，求逆可以得到：
$$
\begin{bmatrix}
\dot{U}_1 \\
\dot{I}_1
\end{bmatrix} =
\begin{bmatrix}
\cos(k x) & j Z_c \sin(k x) \\[4pt]
j\frac{\sin(k x)}{Z_c} & \cos(k x)
\end{bmatrix}
\begin{bmatrix}
\dot U_2 \\
\dot I_2
\end{bmatrix}
$$
