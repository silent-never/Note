	- 设转子d轴相对于定子a相磁链方向的转角为$\theta$。
电势方程：
	$$
	 \begin{bmatrix} u_{\mathrm{a}} \\ u_{\mathrm{b}} \\ u_{\mathrm{c}} \\ \hline -u_{\mathrm{f}} \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} = - \begin{bmatrix} \dot{\psi}_{\mathrm{a}} \\ \dot{\psi}_{\mathrm{b}} \\ \dot{\psi}_{\mathrm{c}} \\ \dot{\psi}_{\mathrm{f}} \\ \dot{\psi}_{\mathrm{D}} \\ \dot{\psi}_{\mathrm{Q}} \end{bmatrix} - \begin{bmatrix} \begin{array}{ccc|ccc} r & 0 & 0 & & & \\ 0 & r & 0 & & \mathbf{0} & \\ 0 & 0 & r & & & \\ \hline & & & r_{\mathrm{f}} & 0 & 0 \\ & \mathbf{0} & & 0 & r_{\mathrm{D}} & 0 \\ & & & 0 & 0 & r_{\mathrm{Q}} \end{array} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} i_{\mathrm{a}} \\ i_{\mathrm{b}} \\ i_{\mathrm{c}} \\ i_{\mathrm{f}} \\ i_{\mathrm{D}} \\ i_{\mathrm{Q}} \end{bmatrix} 
	$$
	即
	$$
 \begin{bmatrix} \boldsymbol{u}_{\mathrm{abc}} \\ \boldsymbol{u}_{\mathrm{fDQ}} \end{bmatrix} = - \begin{bmatrix} \dot{\boldsymbol{\psi}}_{\mathrm{abc}} \\ \dot{\boldsymbol{\psi}}_{\mathrm{fDQ}} \end{bmatrix} - \begin{bmatrix} \boldsymbol{r}_{\mathrm{S}} & \mathbf{0} \\ \mathbf{0} & \boldsymbol{r}_{\mathrm{R}} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} \boldsymbol{i}_{\mathrm{abc}} \\ \boldsymbol{i}_{\mathrm{fDQ}} \end{bmatrix} 
	$$
	其中
		$u_{abc}$：   定子$a,b,c$三相对中心线电压
		$i_{abc}$：    定子$a,b,c$三相流出电流
		$r_s$：      定子$a,b,c$三相绕组电阻
		$u_{fDQ}$： 转子励磁$f$、阻尼$D,Q$回路电动势
		$i_{fDQ}$：  转子励磁$f$、阻尼$D,Q$回路电流
		$r_{fDQ}$：  转子励磁$f$、阻尼$D,Q$回路绕组电阻
		$\psi_{abc},\psi_{fDQ}$：  电流正向流过线圈时所产生的磁链

磁链方程：
	$$
	 \begin{bmatrix} \psi_{\mathrm{a}} \\ \psi_{\mathrm{b}} \\ \psi_{\mathrm{c}} \\ \hdashline \psi_{\mathrm{f}} \\ \psi_{\mathrm{D}} \\ \psi_{\mathrm{Q}} \end{bmatrix} = \left[\begin{array}{ccc|ccc} L_{\mathrm{aa}} & L_{\mathrm{ab}} & L_{\mathrm{ac}} & L_{\mathrm{af}} & L_{\mathrm{aD}} & L_{\mathrm{aQ}} \\ L_{\mathrm{ba}} & L_{\mathrm{bb}} & L_{\mathrm{bc}} & L_{\mathrm{bf}} & L_{\mathrm{bD}} & L_{\mathrm{bQ}} \\ L_{\mathrm{ca}} & L_{\mathrm{cb}} & L_{\mathrm{cc}} & L_{\mathrm{cf}} & L_{\mathrm{cD}} & L_{\mathrm{cQ}} \\ \hline L_{\mathrm{fa}} & L_{\mathrm{fb}} & L_{\mathrm{fc}} & L_{\mathrm{ff}} & L_{\mathrm{fD}} & L_{\mathrm{fQ}} \\ L_{\mathrm{Da}} & L_{\mathrm{Db}} & L_{\mathrm{Dc}} & L_{\mathrm{Df}} & L_{\mathrm{DD}} & L_{\mathrm{DQ}} \\ L_{\mathrm{Qa}} & L_{\mathrm{Qb}} & L_{\mathrm{Qc}} & L_{\mathrm{Qf}} & L_{\mathrm{QD}} & L_{\mathrm{QQ}} \end{array}\right] \begin{bmatrix} i_{\mathrm{a}} \\ i_{\mathrm{b}} \\ i_{\mathrm{c}} \\ \hdashline i_{\mathrm{f}} \\ i_{\mathrm{D}} \\ i_{\mathrm{Q}} \end{bmatrix} 
	$$
	即$$
	\begin{bmatrix} \boldsymbol{\psi}_{\mathrm{abc}} \\ \boldsymbol{\psi}_{\mathrm{fDQ}} \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \boldsymbol{L}_{\mathrm{SS}} & \boldsymbol{L}_{\mathrm{SR}} \\ \boldsymbol{L}_{\mathrm{RS}} & \boldsymbol{L}_{\mathrm{RR}} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} \boldsymbol{i}_{\mathrm{abc}} \\ \boldsymbol{i}_{\mathrm{fDQ}} \end{bmatrix}
	$$
	取$\lambda_{ad},\lambda_{aq}$为沿d轴和q轴方向气隙磁通路径的磁导，
		认为铁芯处磁阻为零，
		该磁导为对该轴为余弦形式的磁势所产生磁通时的等效磁导
	注意到，由于转子转动，气隙磁导处于周期性时变，则与转子气隙相运动的定子线圈的自感互感均处于周期性时变。
	p .s. 相量法的使用：
			不止对于周期性时变函数，类似的周期性空间函数同样可以使用相量法：$$
				\begin{aligned} 
				F_{(\theta)} &= F^0_{(t)}\cos\theta 
				= F^0\cos(\theta-\theta_0+\theta_0)
				\\ &= F^0\cos(\theta_0)\cos(\theta-\theta_0)-F^0\sin(\theta_0)\sin(\theta-\theta_0)
				\\ &= F^0_{d}\cos(\theta-\theta_0)+F^0_{q}\cos(\theta-\theta_0+90^\circ)
				\end{aligned}
			$$
			即空间中余弦分布的场$F_{(\theta)}$可以视为两个$F^0_{d},F^0_{q}$余弦分布的场的叠加，其中$F^0_{q}$以落后$F^0_{d}$ 90°为正方向，
			在空间上符合矢量平行四边形合成法则。
			![[空间场的相量法分解图示.png|270]]
	1、定子各绕组的自感：
		当：$\alpha_{=(a,b,c)}$相有电流$i_\alpha$时，产生空间中余弦分布的磁势：$$ \begin{aligned} F_\alpha(\theta_{\text{space}}) &= w_\alpha i_\alpha(\omega t) \cos(\theta_{\text{space}} - \theta_\alpha) \\ &= F_\alpha \cos(\theta_{\text{space}} - \theta_\alpha-\theta+\theta) 
		\\ &= F_\alpha \cos(\theta-\theta_\alpha)\cos(\theta_{\text{space}} -\theta) +F_\alpha \sin(\theta-\theta_\alpha)\cos(\theta_{\text{space}} -\theta+90^\circ)
		\end{aligned} $$可以将磁势$F_\alpha$分解为$d$轴分量与$q$轴分量：$F_{\alpha d}=F_\alpha \cos(\theta-\theta_\alpha),F_{\alpha q}=F_\alpha \sin(\theta-\theta_\alpha)$
		则磁势$F_\alpha$在$d$轴与$q$轴产生余弦分布的磁通：$$
		\left. \begin{aligned} \Phi_{\mathrm{\alpha d}} &= \lambda_{\mathrm{ad}} F_\alpha \cos(\theta-\theta_\alpha) \\ \Phi_{\mathrm{\alpha q}} &= \lambda_{\mathrm{aq}}F_\alpha \sin(\theta-\theta_\alpha) \end{aligned} \right\}
		$$其中磁通也可以用相量分解的方式拆分成各相绕组上的磁通
		此时与$\alpha$相绕组相交链的磁链为：$w_\alpha$为$\alpha$相绕组的等效匝数、$\lambda_{s\sigma}$为定子漏磁磁导$$$\begin{align*} \psi_{\alpha\alpha} &= w_{\alpha} \left( \Phi_{\sigma\alpha} + \Phi_{\alpha d} \cos(\theta-\theta_\alpha)) + \Phi_{\alpha q} \sin(\theta-\theta_\alpha) \right) \\ &= w_{\alpha}^2 i_{\alpha} \left( \lambda_{s\sigma} + \lambda_{ad} \cos^2(\theta-\theta_\alpha) + \lambda_{aq} \sin^2(\theta-\theta_\alpha) \right) \\\end{align*}$$则其自感为：$$ \begin{aligned} L_{\alpha\alpha} = \frac{\psi_{\alpha\alpha}}{i_{\alpha}} &= w_{\alpha}^2 \left( \lambda_{s\sigma} + \lambda_{ad}\cos^2(\theta-\theta_\alpha) + \lambda_{aq}\sin^2(\theta-\theta_\alpha) \right) \\ &= l_0 + l_2\cos2(\theta-\theta_\alpha) \\ & \left\{ \begin{aligned} l_0 &= w_{\alpha}^2 \left( \lambda_{s\sigma} + \frac{1}{2}(\lambda_{ad}+\lambda_{aq}) \right) \\ l_2 &= \frac{1}{2}w_{\alpha}^2 (\lambda_{ad}-\lambda_{aq}) \end{aligned} \right. \end{aligned} $$其中：可以取$$\left\{\begin{aligned}\theta_{\alpha=a} &= 0^\circ \\\theta_{\alpha=b} &= 120^\circ \\\theta_{\alpha=c} &= -120^\circ\end{aligned}\right.$$
	2、定子各绕组的互感：
		当$\alpha_{=(a,b,c)}$相有电流$i_\alpha$时，对$\beta_{=(a,b,c)}(\beta \neq \alpha)$ 相磁链有贡献：$$ \begin{aligned} \psi_{\beta\alpha} &= w_\beta \left[ \Phi_{\beta\alpha\sigma} + \Phi_{\alpha d} \cos\left(\theta - \theta_\beta\right) + \Phi_{\alpha q} \sin\left(\theta - \theta_\beta\right) \right] \\ &= w_\alpha w_\beta i_\alpha \left[ -\lambda_{m\sigma} + \lambda_{ad} \cos(\theta - \theta_\alpha)\cos\left(\theta - \theta_\beta\right) + \lambda_{\alpha q} \sin(\theta - \theta_\alpha) \sin\left(\theta - \theta_\beta\right) \right] \end{aligned} $$取定子各相绕组的等效匝数相同$w_\alpha=w$，则$$ \begin{aligned} L_{\alpha\beta} = L_{\beta\alpha} = \frac{\psi_{\beta\alpha}}{i_\alpha} &= w^2 \Bigg[ -\lambda_{m\sigma} + \frac{1}{2}(\lambda_{ad}+\lambda_{aq})\cos(\theta_\beta-\theta_\alpha) + \frac{1}{2}(\lambda_{ad}-\lambda_{aq})\cos[2\theta - \theta_\alpha - \theta_\beta]  \Bigg] 
		\\ &= -m_0 + m_2\cos[2\theta - \theta_\alpha - \theta_\beta] 
		\\ &\quad \left\{ \begin{aligned} 
		m_0 &= w^2 \left[ \lambda_{m\sigma} -  \frac{1}{2}(\lambda_{ad}+\lambda_{aq})\cos(\theta_\beta-\theta_\alpha) \right] 
		\\ m_2 &= \frac{1}{2}w^2 (\lambda_{ad} - \lambda_{aq}) \end{aligned} \right. \end{aligned} $$其中有$$ \begin{aligned} m_0 &= w^2 \left[ \lambda_{m\sigma} -  \frac{1}{2}(\lambda_{ad}+\lambda_{aq})\cos(\theta_\beta-\theta_\alpha) \right] \\&=  w^2(\lambda_{m\sigma}+\frac{1}{4}(\lambda_{ad}+\lambda_{aq}))=const\end{aligned}$$
	3、定子与转子绕组间的互感：
		当定子绕组$\alpha_{=(a,b,c)}$相有电流$i_\alpha$时，对转子绕组$\gamma_{=(f,D)}$ 磁链有贡献：$$\psi_{\alpha\gamma}=ww_\gamma i_\alpha\lambda_{ad}\cos(\theta-\theta_\alpha)$$所以$$\begin{aligned} L_{\alpha\gamma}=\frac{\psi_{\alpha\gamma}}{i_\alpha} &=ww_\gamma \lambda_{ad}\cos(\theta-\theta_\alpha) \\&=m_{\alpha\gamma}\cos(\theta-\theta_\alpha)\end{aligned}$$而由于$q$轴落后于$d$轴90°，故：$$\begin{aligned} L_{\alpha Q}=\frac{\psi_{\alpha Q}}{i_\alpha} &=ww_Q \lambda_{aq}\sin(\theta-\theta_\alpha) \\&=m_{\alpha Q}\sin(\theta-\theta_\alpha)\end{aligned}$$
	4、而转子各绕组的自感和互感为固定值：
		$励磁f//D轴⊥Q轴$，等效匝比分别为$w_f,w_D,w_Q$，漏磁磁导分别为$\lambda_{\sigma f},\lambda_{\sigma D},\lambda_{\sigma Q}$：  $$ \begin{bmatrix} L_{ff} & L_{fD} & L_{fQ} \\ L_{Df} & L_{DD} & L_{DQ} \\ L_{Qf} & L_{QD} & L_{QQ}\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} w_f^2(\lambda_{\sigma f}+\lambda_{ad}) & w_f w_D\lambda_{ad} & 0 \\ w_f w_D\lambda_{ad} & w_D^2(\lambda_{\sigma D}+\lambda_{ad}) & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & w_Q^2(\lambda_{\sigma Q}+\lambda_{ad}) \end{bmatrix} $$
	故总电感矩阵为：$$
		\mathbf{L} = \begin{bmatrix}
		l_0 + l_2\cos2\theta & 
		-m_0 + m_2\cos(2\theta-120^\circ) & 
		-m_0 + m_2\cos(2\theta+120^\circ) & 
		w w_f \lambda_{ad}\cos\theta & 
		w w_D \lambda_{ad}\cos\theta & 
		w w_Q \lambda_{aq}\sin\theta \\[6pt]
		-m_0 + m_2\cos(2\theta-120^\circ) & 
		l_0 + l_2\cos2(\theta-120^\circ) & 
		-m_0 + m_2\cos2\theta & 
		w w_f \lambda_{ad}\cos(\theta-120^\circ) & 
		w w_D \lambda_{ad}\cos(\theta-120^\circ) & 
		w w_Q \lambda_{aq}\sin(\theta-120^\circ) \\[6pt]
		-m_0 + m_2\cos(2\theta+120^\circ) & 
		-m_0 + m_2\cos2\theta & 
		l_0 + l_2\cos2(\theta+120^\circ) & 
		w w_f \lambda_{ad}\cos(\theta+120^\circ) & 
		w w_D \lambda_{ad}\cos(\theta+120^\circ) & 
		w w_Q \lambda_{aq}\sin(\theta+120^\circ) \\[6pt]
		w w_f \lambda_{ad}\cos\theta & 
		w w_f \lambda_{ad}\cos(\theta-120^\circ) & 
		w w_f \lambda_{ad}\cos(\theta+120^\circ) & 
		w_f^2(\lambda_{\sigma f}+\lambda_{ad}) & 
		w_f w_D \lambda_{ad} & 0 \\[6pt]
		w w_D \lambda_{ad}\cos\theta & 
		w w_D \lambda_{ad}\cos(\theta-120^\circ) & 
		w w_D \lambda_{ad}\cos(\theta+120^\circ) & 
		w_f w_D \lambda_{ad} & 
		w_D^2(\lambda_{\sigma D}+\lambda_{ad}) & 0 \\[6pt]
		w w_Q \lambda_{aq}\sin\theta & 
		w w_Q \lambda_{aq}\sin(\theta-120^\circ) & 
		w w_Q \lambda_{aq}\sin(\theta+120^\circ) & 
		0 & 0 & 
		w_Q^2(\lambda_{\sigma Q}+\lambda_{aq})
		\end{bmatrix}
		$$其中$$
		\left\{
		\begin{aligned}
		l_0 &= w_{\alpha}^2 \left( \lambda_{s\sigma} + \frac{1}{2}(\lambda_{ad}+\lambda_{aq}) \right) \\
		l_2 &= \frac{1}{2} w_{\alpha}^2 (\lambda_{ad}-\lambda_{aq}) \\
		m_0 &= w^2 \left( \lambda_{m\sigma} + \frac{1}{4}(\lambda_{ad}+\lambda_{aq}) \right) \\
		m_2 &= \frac{1}{2} w^2 (\lambda_{ad} - \lambda_{aq})
		\end{aligned}
		\right.
		$$
