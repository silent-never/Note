有名值：$$ \left. \begin{aligned} \frac{\mathrm{d}\delta}{\mathrm{d}t} &= \omega - \omega_{\mathrm{N}}  \\ \Omega J\frac{\mathrm{d}\Omega}{\mathrm{d}t} &= P_m-P_e-D\frac{\omega-\omega_{\mathrm{N}}}{\omega_{\mathrm{N}}}\end{aligned} \right\} $$
标幺值：
	由于$$ \omega_* = \frac{\omega}{\omega_{\mathrm{N}}}, \quad t_* = \omega_{\mathrm{N}} t, \quad P_* = \frac{P}{S_{\mathrm{B}}} $$代入得到：$$ \left. \begin{aligned} \omega_{\mathrm{N}} \frac{\mathrm{d}\delta}{\mathrm{d}t_*} &= \omega_{\mathrm{N}} \left( \omega_* - 1 \right) \\ J\Omega_{\mathrm{N}}^2 \omega_{\mathrm{N}} \frac{\mathrm{d}\Omega_*}{\mathrm{d}t_*} &= S_{\mathrm{B}} \left( P_{m*} - P_{e*} - D_*(\omega_* - 1) \right) \end{aligned} \right\} $$又由于$$ \omega = p\Omega, \quad \omega_{\mathrm{N}} = p\Omega_{\mathrm{N}} ,\quad \rightarrow \omega_* =\Omega_*$$整理可得：$$
\left.
\begin{aligned}
\frac{\mathrm{d}\delta}{\mathrm{d}t_*} &= \omega_* - 1 \\
T_{\mathrm{J}} \omega_{\mathrm{N}} \frac{\mathrm{d}\omega_*}{\mathrm{d}t_*} &= P_{m*} - P_{e*} - D_*(\omega_* - 1)
\end{aligned}
\right\}
		$$其中$$T_{\mathrm{J}} = \frac{J\Omega_{\mathrm{N}}^2}{S_{\mathrm{B}}} , \quad P_* = \frac{P}{S_{\mathrm{B}}},\quad D_* = \frac{D}{S_{\mathrm{B}}}$$

---

惯性时间常数：（时间非标幺）
$$
T_{\mathrm{J}} \frac{\mathrm{d}\omega_*}{\mathrm{d}t} = \Delta M_*
$$
	对时间积分，即转子从零开始施加额定转矩 $\Delta M_*=1$ ，达到额定转速 $\omega_*=\Omega_*=1$ 的时间
	对于多机系统，各发电机同步运行，等式左右为功率，故利用标幺值合并：
$$
	T_{J\Sigma}=\frac{\sum T_{JNi}S_{Ni}}{S_B}
$$

---

转速、时间非标幺：
	鉴于分析小扰动状态方程来求解系统震荡频率时，希望所求出的特征根带有正确的$s^{-1}$量纲，故取时间非标幺化处理：$$ \omega_* = \frac{\omega}{\omega_{\mathrm{N}}}, \quad T_{\mathrm{J}} = \frac{J\Omega_{\mathrm{N}}^2}{S_{\mathrm{B}}} , \quad P_* = \frac{P}{S_{\mathrm{B}}},\quad D_* = \frac{D}{S_{\mathrm{B}}} $$并且所有的状态变量携带有正确的时间量纲，将其余量纲标幺化入参数中处理：$$ \left. \begin{aligned} \frac{\mathrm{d}\delta}{\mathrm{d}t} &= \omega - \omega_{\mathrm{N}}  \\ \frac{T_{\mathrm{J}}}{\omega_{\mathrm{N}}}\frac{\mathrm{d}\omega}{\mathrm{d}t} &= P_{m*}-P_{e*}-\frac{D_*}{\omega_\mathrm{N}}(\omega-\omega_{\mathrm{N}})\end{aligned} \right\} $$写成线性化的小扰动方程$$\left.
\begin{aligned}
\frac{\mathrm{d}\Delta\delta}{\mathrm{d}t} &= \Delta \omega \\
\frac{T_{\mathrm{J}}}{\omega_{\mathrm{N}}} \frac{\mathrm{d}\Delta\omega}{\mathrm{d}t} &=  - S_{Eq}\Delta\delta - D_*'\Delta \omega
\end{aligned}
\right\}$$其中$$S_{Eq}=\frac{\mathrm{d}P_{e*}}{\mathrm{d}\delta}\Big|_{\delta=\delta_0,\;E_{q}=const},\quad D_*'=\frac{D_*}{\omega_\mathrm{N}}$$
