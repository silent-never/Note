以 $q$ 轴为坐标轴，$E\angle 0^\circ,\; U\angle -\delta,\;I\angle -\delta-\varphi$

隐极机：$X_{q\Sigma}=X_{d\Sigma}=X_{\Sigma}$
$$
\dot E_q=\dot U +j\,X_{\Sigma}\dot I
$$

凸极机：$X_{d\Sigma},\,X_{q\Sigma}$
$$
\begin{aligned}
\dot E_q&=\dot U +j\,X_{d\Sigma}\dot I_d+j\,X_{q\Sigma}\dot I_q  \\
\dot I&=\dot I_d+\dot I_q
\end{aligned}
$$
	注意到，电流与电势之间的相角只被 $j\,X_{q\Sigma}\dot I_q$ 作用，故采取与 $\dot E$ 同相位的计算用电势
$$
\begin{aligned} 
\dot E_Q &=\dot U +j\,X_{q\Sigma}(\dot I_d +\dot I_q )\\
&=\dot U +j\,X_{q\Sigma}\dot I \\
\dot E_q &= \dot E_Q +j\,(X_{d\Sigma}-X_{q\Sigma})\dot I_d
\end{aligned}
$$

发电机端电压：$U_G$
$$
\begin{aligned} 
\dot U_G &=\dot U +j\,X_{TL}(\dot I_d +\dot I_q )\\
&=\dot U +j\,X_{TL}\dot I \\
\dot E_q &= \dot U_G +j\,X_{d}\dot I_d+j\,X_{q}\dot I_q 
\end{aligned}
$$
$$
\begin{aligned} 
\dot U_{Gq} &=\dot U +j\,X_{TL}\dot I_d+j\,X_{q\Sigma}\dot I_q \\
\dot E_q &= \dot U_{Gq} +j\,X_{d}\dot I_d 
\end{aligned}
$$
