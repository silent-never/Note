- **电压降落 (Voltage Drop)**：元件首端与末端电压的**相量差**：$\Delta \dot{U}+\delta \dot{U} = \dot{U}_1 - \dot{U}_2$
	- 纵分量：$\Delta \dot{U}\; \parallel \dot{U}$
	- 横分量：$\delta \dot{U}\;\perp \dot{U}$
	- 注意到，对于 $\dot{U}_1$ 和 $\dot{U}_2$ 的纵分量和横分量是不一样的 $\Delta \dot U_1+\delta \dot U_1=\Delta \dot U_2+\delta \dot U_2$
- 求 $\Delta \dot U_2\;\delta \dot U_2$：
	- 取电压 $\dot{U}_2=U_2\angle 0 ^\circ, \quad \dot U_1=U_1\angle \delta ,\quad \dot I=I\angle -\varphi_2$
	- 有$$\dot{U}_1 - \dot{U}_2 = \Delta U_2+j\;\delta U_2=(R + \mathrm{j}X)\dot{I}$$
	- 分离实部虚部有：$$\left.
\begin{aligned}
\Delta U_2 &= R I \cos\varphi_2 + X I \sin\varphi_2 \\
\delta U_2 &= X I \cos\varphi_2 - R I \sin\varphi_2
\end{aligned}
\right\}$$
	- 如果使用功率表示：$\dot I=\frac{S_2}{U_2}^*$ $$\left.
\begin{aligned}
\Delta U_2 &= \frac{P_2R + Q_2X}{U_2} \\
\delta U_2 &= \frac{P_2X - Q_2R}{U_2}
\end{aligned}
\right\}$$
- 求 $\Delta \dot U_1\;\delta \dot U_1$：
	- 取电压 $\dot{U}_1=U_1\angle 0 ^\circ, \quad \dot U_2=U_2\angle -\delta$
	- 有 $$\dot{U}_1 - \dot{U}_2 = \Delta U_1+j\;\delta U_1=(R + \mathrm{j}X)\dot{I}$$
	- 如果使用功率表示：$\dot I=\frac{S_1}{U_1}^*$ $$\left.
\begin{aligned}
\Delta U_2 &= \frac{P_1R + Q_1X}{U_1} \\
\delta U_2 &= \frac{P_1X - Q_1R}{U_1}
\end{aligned}
\right\}$$
